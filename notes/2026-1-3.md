**Why People Don’t Trust AI and What To Do About It** from Andrew's Letters, The Batch

In the context of America, many distrust the use of AI. There is a need to address this because this lack of trust hampers the progress of AI.

A positive sentiment towards AI is an advantage. Alternatively, a widespread distrust leads to slow adoption, hindered progress, laws that further hamper AI progress.

The solution?
1. Build people’s trust through training.
2. Avoid hyping things up and fear mongering

There are still many problems brought by AI, so the community must work hard to solve these problems to gain society’s trust.
